OUR PHILOSOPHY

Doing good is a state of mind that can accompany every action and shine through every thought. Doing good starts from within, so even the smallest action can help the planet we all inhabit. Ultimately, we believe that a critical mass of people who want good, think good, and do good can generate positive transformation in the world. The crucial moment to start is now!

The natural world is under threat. In the Amazon, more than 150 acres of rainforest are lost every minute of every day. Populations of mammals, birds, fish, amphibians, and reptiles have seen an alarming average drop of 68% since 1970. And this isn't just about the natural world - it's about our world as well. We depend on nature for our own survival, livelihoods, happiness, and as seen with COVID-19, our health - the destruction of nature only increases the chances of future pandemics emerging. Our broken relationship with nature is putting the future of people and the planet at risk. But humanity is waking up...and stepping up. A growing number of people around the world are concerned about humanity's relationship with nature, changing their ways, and demanding more action to protect the planet. We want to do help you do your bit, to join the change movement. At Eco - Tips we provide easy every day life hacks that helps you do your best, for the planet and for all of us.
